SUSHI BAKE

Sushi bake is a deconstructed, baked casserole version of a sushi roll, typically inspired by the California roll.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Imitation Crab
  • Japanese Mayonaise
  • Furikake
  • Nori Sheets

Instructions

  1. Prep Rice: Cook sushi rice and season lightly. Press it firmly into a baking dish.
  2. Mix Topping: In a bowl, combine the shredded imitation crab and mayonnaise until creamy.
  3. Assemble: Sprinkle a generous layer of furikake over the rice. Spread the crab mixture evenly on top.
  4. Bake: Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 15-20 minutes until the top is bubbly and slightly golden. For extra crispiness, broil for the last 2-3 minutes.
  5. Serve: Scoop portions onto nori sheets and eat like a hand roll.
